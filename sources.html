Annotated bibliography

These are the strongest Ghana-specific sources for this project. I prioritized peer-reviewed scholarship, university research repositories, legal texts, and Ghanaian official statistics.

Nimrita Kaur Rana

Being and Becoming: Sindhis in Accra, Ghana. PhD thesis, University of Birmingham, 2020.

Best single source for Ghana-specific community history, migration, identity, and associational life. Strongest on Sindhi history in Accra.

Open thesis

Ghana Statistical Service

2021 Population and Housing Census: General Report Volume 3C.

Best official modern demographic baseline. Important limitation: the category is “Asia,” not a clean count of South Asians.

Open report

Adjei Adjepong

The Origins, Implementation and Effects of Ghana’s 1969 Aliens Compliance Order. MPhil thesis, University of Cape Coast, 2009.

Best Ghana-specific source for the policy history behind the 1969 order and its effects on immigrant communities.

Open thesis

Ghanaian Business (Promotion) Act, 1970

Primary legal text, Act 334.

Use this when discussing retail restrictions and Ghanaianisation in exact legal terms.

Act 334 PDF

Tracy Mensah

“Nkrumah’s ‘Industrial Middlemen’: Sindhi Manufacturers, Expatriate Capital, and Development in Ghana, 1951–1966.” African Economic History, 2023.

Best source for the shift from trade to manufacturing and for linking Sindhi business history to postcolonial development.

Journal page

Shobana Shankar

“Singing and Sensing the Unknown: An Embodied History of Hindu Devotion in Ghana.”

Excellent for religion, the Hindu Monastery of Africa, and the interaction between Ghanaian Hindu life and Indian-linked religious worlds.

Accessible copy

How to cite the site in your presentation

You can say that the website is based primarily on university research, peer-reviewed economic history, Ghanaian census data, and Ghanaian legal texts. That phrasing makes the site sound research-driven rather than decorative.

For an oral presentation, mention Rana, Mensah, Ghana Statistical Service, and Adjepong first. Those are the most useful anchor sources.
